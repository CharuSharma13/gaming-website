    SOSTRONK LAUNCHES DELHI SERVER

    It’s been a long time coming, and it’s finally happened! We’re happy to announce that we have launched SoStronk servers in New Delhi for our North Indian user base. We know we’ve had a massive number of users from up north in the country who are dedicated users and have been so for years. This is our little thank you to all of you by giving you that low ping goodness. It took us a while to find the best possible server partner in order to give the best ping and performance for you. However, if you face any issues playing on our new servers, let us know on our forums Now get fragging!

  • Nov 3, 2019

    SOSTRONK TIES UP WITH TRYHARD.GG

    Playing on SoStronk is about trying to be the best. Where the crème de la crème of Indian CS:GO battle each other to be king. With so many players trying as hard as possible to top the leaderboards, it’s only fitting that we partner up with tryhard.gg ! Tryhard.gg is an esports rewards platform aimed at giving casual everyday gamers an opportunity to be rewarded by participating in leagues of their favorite online games. Be it single player or team games, players are scored and ranked based on their individual performances, and are rewarded accordingly, irrespective of the end result of the game.
